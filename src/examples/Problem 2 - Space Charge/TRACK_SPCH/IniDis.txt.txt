
  Types of Initial particles distributions generated by TRACK using "iflag_dis" in "track.dat"

  iflag_dis = 0: 4D Waterbag

            = 1: 6D Waterbag

            = 2: 4D Uniform (Not yet available)

            = 3: 6D Uniform

            = 4: 2D Gaussian, truncated at 3*sigma, X and Y independent

            = 5: 4D Gaussian, truncated at 3*sigma

            = 6: 6D Gaussian, truncated at 3*sigma

            = 7: 2D Uniform, X and Y independent

            = 8: 4D KV distribution

            = 9: 4D Free Gaussian, truncated at 3*sigma
